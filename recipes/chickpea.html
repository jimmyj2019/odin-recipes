Cheat's chickpea korma curry

Asking yourself what's for dinner? Make it easy,
by cooking this cheat's chickpea korma curry recipe that's loaded with veggies for a wholesome meat-free meal.

5 Ingredients

  • 500g pkt Coles Roasting Vegetables with Garlic & Rosemary
  • 400g can Coles Chickpeas, rinsed, drained
  • 375g pkt creamy korma simmer sauce
  • 2 x 250g Coles Microwave Basmati Rice Pouches
  • 1/3 cup coriander sprigs

2 Method Steps

  1. Chop the vegetables and garlic from the roasting kit into 2cm
    pieces. (Reserve rosemary for another use.) Heat a large non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Add the vegetable mixture
    and chickpeas and cook, stirring, for 5 mins or until the onion softens.
  2. Add the korma sauce and 1/2 cup (125ml) water to the
    vegetable mixture in the pan. Bring to the boil. Reduce heat to low. Cover and cook, stirring, for 5 mins. Uncover. Cook,
    stirring, for a further 5 mins or until the vegetables are tender and the sauce thickens slightly.
